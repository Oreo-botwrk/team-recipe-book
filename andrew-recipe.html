Artisinal Bread

By: Andrew Camarillo-Bergum

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups lukewarm water
  • 1 tbsp granulated yeast
  • 1 - 1 1/2 tbsp kosher salt
  • 6 1/2 cups all-purpose flour

Instructions:

  1. In 5 or 6 quart bowl or lidded dough bucket, pur in water, and add yeast and salt
  2. Dump in flour all at once and stir with ling handled widden spoon or Danish Dough Whisk
  3. Stir until four is incorperated into the dough, it will be wet
  4. Put lid on container, do not snap shut so gases can escape
  5. allow dough to rise at room temp for two hours
  6. after dough has risen, dust surfacew on dough with flour to prevent sticking and pull a chunk of dough
  7. place ball of dough on parchment sheet or pizza peel
  8. let dough ball rise for at least 40 minutes
  9. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F, with a metal broiler tray on the bottom
  10. Place loaf in preheated oven and add a cup of hot water to broiler tray
  11. Remove parchment paper after 20-25 minutes to allow bottom to crisp
  12. Let loaf finish baking for 5-10 minutes
  13. Remove Bread from oven and let cool before slicing and serving

Cooking time: 45 minutes

Serves: 4 people (or a very hungry person)
