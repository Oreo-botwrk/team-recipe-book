Grilled Chicken Thighs w/ BBQ Sauce

By: Tim Cole

Ingredients:

  • 2 Boneless, skinless, chicken thighs (from 6-8 ounces)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 cup barbecue sauce

Instructions:

  1. Step 1: Prepare and season chicken. Pat the chicken Thighs dry using paper towels, using a small bowl, mix 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder,
  2. 1/2 teaspoon onion powder, 1/2 teaspoon paprika, 1/4 teaspoon salt, and 1/8 teaspoon black pepper. Rub 1 tablespoon olive oil all over chicken thighs,
  3. then sprinkile the seasoning mixture evenly on both sides. Gently rub the seasoning into the chicken.
  4. Step 2: Heat up Grill: Preheat your outdoor grill to medium-high heat. If using a grill pan indoors, heat it over medium-high heat until it is hot and lightly smoking.
  5. A hot grill prevents sticking and helps create good grill marks.
  6. Step 3: Grill the chicken: Place the seasoned chicken thighs on the hot grill. Cook for 6-8 minutes per side, or until the chicken is cooked through and internal temp reaches 165 degrees farenheit (74 degrees celcius)
  7. The total cooking time will depend on the thickness of the chicken thighs. During the last 2-3 mins of grilling, brush chicken generously with 1/2 cup BBQ sauce on both sides, allowing it to caramelize (turn slightly sticky and browned)
  8. Step 4: Rest and serve. Once the chicken is cooked, take it off the grill and let it rest on a cutting board/plate for 5 mins. Resting allows juices to settle into the meat, keeping it tender and juice, then serve thighs immediately.

Cooking time: 25-30 minutes (10 minutes prep-time, 15-20 minutes cook time)

Serves: 2 people
